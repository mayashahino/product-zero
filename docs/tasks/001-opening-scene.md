# 001 — Opening Scene

## Goal

Create the first 10–15 seconds of Product Zero.

The opening should make the visitor feel that this is not a traditional portfolio, but an experience designed to demonstrate product thinking.

## Core Message

Some things are easier to experience than explain.

## User

Hiring managers / product leaders who have limited time and need to quickly understand whether Maya is worth meeting.

## Desired Feeling

Curiosity → Understanding → Trust → Excitement

## Experience Direction

The opening should feel calm, premium, intelligent and unexpected.

It should suggest the idea of complexity becoming clear, without using obvious metaphors like a puzzle or a literal brain.

## Success Criteria

- Feels original and memorable.
- Encourages scrolling.
- Uses motion with purpose.
- Demonstrates product thinking instead of explaining it.
- Works beautifully on desktop and mobile.
- Does not overwhelm the user.

## Initial Copy

Some things are easier to experience than explain.

## Output Needed

Before writing code, propose 2–3 concept directions.

For each direction include:
- Concept name
- What the visitor sees
- What happens on scroll or interaction
- What idea the motion communicates
- Why it fits Product Zero
- Risks / what could make it feel gimmicky
